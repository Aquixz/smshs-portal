Privacy Statement

Our Online Enrollment System is designed to provide students with a fast, secure, and convenient way to complete their enrollment. To ensure the protection of every user, our system strictly follows data privacy principles and safeguards all personal information submitted.

1. Information We Collect

Our system collects only the data needed to process and confirm enrollment, such as:

  • Student name and basic profile information
  • Contact details (email, phone number)
  • Academic information needed for placement
  • Required digital documents for enrollment verification

We do not collect any unrelated or unnecessary personal information.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Completing and verifying the enrollment process
  • Generating student records and official forms
  • Communicating enrollment updates or requirements
  • Supporting administrative tasks related to admission

Your information will not be used for non-school activities.

3. Data Protection and Security

  • Password-protected accounts for students and staff
  • Secure and encrypted storage of submitted data
  • Limited access for authorized school personnel only
  • Regular system monitoring to prevent unauthorized activities

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be shared with outside organizations, except when:

  • It is required by law or government policy
  • It is needed for official school procedures
  • You (or your parent/guardian) give formal permission

We do not sell or distribute student information to any third party.

5. Data Retention

All information submitted will be stored only for as long as needed for enrollment and record-keeping purposes, following school policies and data privacy regulations.

6. Your Rights as a User

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information stored in the system
  • Request corrections for inaccurate or outdated details
  • Ask how your data is being used
  • Seek help from the system administrator or Data Protection Officer regarding privacy concerns

7. Consent

By using our Online Enrollment System, you confirm that you understand and agree to this Privacy Statement and allow the system to collect and process your information for enrollment purposes.

8. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding data privacy, you may contact our system administrator or school office through the official communication channels provided.
